## Root Cause

ProcessGroupXCCL rejects ReduceOp.PREMUL_SUM because underlying oneCCL does not yet expose the
premul-sum reduction used by FSDP2 reduce_scatter_tensor with a divide factor
(test/distributed/_composable/fsdp/test_fully_shard_comm.py::test_set_reduce_scatter_divide_factor).
Blocked on oneCCL feature MLSL-3345; target PT 2.10.

## Fix Approach

- Once oneCCL exposes PREMUL_SUM, map it in torch/csrc/distributed/c10d/ProcessGroupXCCL (getXcclReduceOp) and remove the ValueError guard.
- re-enable the FSDP test.
- Until then keep skip entry in test/xpu/skip_list_dist.py and close issue when MLSL-3345 lands.
