## Root Cause

torchbench BERT_pytorch amp_bf16 inference on PVC fails dynamo accuracy with RMSE(res-fp64)=0.00196
vs RMSE(ref-fp64)=0.00058 (3.4x ref, tol=0.001). Owner mengfei25 marked this as a duplicate of the
long-standing #1811 BERT bf16 accuracy issue, which traces to bf16 matmul/linear accumulation
precision in the oneDNN-backed path used by BERT's transformer block on PVC. The XPU bf16 GEMM
accumulator/reduction order yields a slightly larger numerical error than the dynamo accuracy
multiplier (3x) tolerates, while ref/eager itself is well within fp64 RMSE bound.

## Fix Approach

- 1) Track upstream resolution of #1811 (oneDNN bf16 accumulation/precision policy) and uprev oneDNN once fixed. 2) Until then, mark BERT_pytorch amp_bf16 inference as a known-failure in the torch-xpu-ops accuracy expected-list (`.ci/benchmarks/`) so 2.12 release nightly is not blocked. 3) Investigate whether forcing fp32 accumulation in the oneDNN matmul primitive descriptor (post-op accumulator dtype) closes the gap on PVC.
