## Root Cause

test/distributed/_tools/test_runtime_estimator.py, test_sac_ilp.py and test_sac_estimator.py are
skipped on XPU because torch/distributed/_tools/sac_estimator.py and runtime_estimator.py, plus
torch/_inductor/analysis/device_info.py and _inductor/utils.py, are CUDA-hardcoded with no
XPU/Accelerator branch. Triton's python/triton/testing.py:421 also lacks XPU support. Even after
hacks, peak_mem assertion fails (2.52 vs 1.0).
