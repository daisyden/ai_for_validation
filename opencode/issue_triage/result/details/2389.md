## Root Cause

test_cond_errors_and_warnings_xpu_complex128 (test/test_linalg.py:1719) flakily reports 'Data
corruption detected' or 'Expected 1 warning but got 2' from torch.linalg.cond on complex128. The
extra warning is emitted by the XPU linalg path (oneMKL-backed SVD via
aten/src/ATen/native/xpu/linalg/* and torch-xpu-ops linalg svd kernels) doing an extra internal
copyback/resize on the user-provided out tensor, producing a duplicate UserWarning. Issue is
non-deterministic across CI workers (labeled 'random').

## Fix Approach

- Trace warning generation in torch.linalg.cond out= path on XPU.
- suppress the redundant resize/copy warning when the provided out tensor already matches the result shape/dtype (analogous to fixes done for CUDA).
- Likely fix in pytorch upstream torch/_linalg_utils or aten/src/ATen/native/LinearAlgebra.cpp warning emission.
- cross-check oneMKL svd wrapper in torch-xpu-ops src/ATen/native/xpu/linalg/.
