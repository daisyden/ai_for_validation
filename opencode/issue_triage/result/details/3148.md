## Root Cause

openai/whisper-tiny inductor bf16 inference accuracy regresses (RMSE res-fp64 0.0445 vs ref-fp64
0.0110, tol 0.001) only when switching intel-xpu-backend-for-triton from 64bb0de3 to
3.7.1+git21033c4e on PyTorch release/2.11. The bug is a Triton codegen/lowering regression in the
new commit range, not in torch-xpu-ops; root cause is tracked at
intel/intel-xpu-backend-for-triton#6489. Workaround proposal in comments is to enlarge tolerance for
whisper-tiny in benchmarks/dynamo/huggingface.py.

## Fix Approach

- Wait for fix in intel-xpu-backend-for-triton#6489 and bump the triton pin once landed.
- As short-term mitigation, raise the whisper-tiny tolerance multiplier in the HF dynamo benchmark suite (per chuanqi129's suggestion) and bisect the 64bb0de3..21033c4e Triton range to identify the offending pass.
