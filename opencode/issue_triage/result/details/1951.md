## Root Cause

Bucket of TestCommonXPU.test_out failures with multiple distinct root causes. (a)
native_batch_norm/_native_batch_norm_legit hits TORCH_INTERNAL_ASSERT at
third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/BatchNormKernels.cpp:595-599 because the out=
path passes a 0-element/non-contiguous out_invstd that the kernel doesn't resize before asserting
(resize_output is called but the assert checks contiguous/sizes()[0] != 0). (b)
addmv/mv/triangular_solve/cholesky_inverse/geqrf/ormqr stride mismatch — out= path forces contiguous
output instead of preserving original strides (oneMKL/oneDNN-backed ops). (c) histc fails to raise
the expected RuntimeError on unsafe float32->long cast — error-checking missing in HistogramKernel.
(d) avg_pool2d numeric mismatch on out= path. (e) narrow_copy stride/contiguity issue.

## Fix Approach

- Split into sub-issues. (a) Fix BatchNormKernels.cpp:595 by relaxing the assertion or ensuring resize_output produces a 1-D contiguous tensor before assert, and skip assert when out_invstd is provided empty. (b) For addmv/mv/triangular_solve/cholesky_inverse/geqrf/ormqr (oneMKL paths): preserve out tensor strides rather than forcing contiguity in the wrapper. (c) Add unsafe-cast check in HistogramKernel out= path. (d) Investigate avg_pool2d non-contiguous out=. (e) narrow_copy stride preservation.
