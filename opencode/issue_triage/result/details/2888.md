## Root Cause

`test/inductor/test_fp8.py::TestFP8TypesXPU.test_bad_cast_xpu` triggers an assertion in
`torch/_inductor/codegen/triton.py:1086` (`_get_min_elements_per_thread`) that bans direct
float8_e5m2↔float8_e4m3fn conversion. This guard was added for CUDA/Triton because nvgpu Triton has
no native fp8↔fp8 conversion; XPU/Intel-Triton inherits the same assert path but should either
support or graceful-fallback the cast. The Inductor lowering does not insert a fp16/bf16 staging
cast for XPU as it does for some other unsupported direct conversions.

## Fix Approach

- Either (a) upstream patch in `torch/_inductor/codegen/triton.py` to relax the guard for XPU and emit a staged cast through fp16/bf16 for fp8_e5m2↔fp8_e4m3fn on Intel Triton, or (b) lower the cast in Inductor decomposition before reaching Triton codegen.
- Coordinate with intel-xpu-backend-for-triton on direct fp8↔fp8 support.
- assigned to @Stonepia per comment with June target.
