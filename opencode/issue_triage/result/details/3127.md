## Root Cause

test_math_backend_high_precision at test_transformers_xpu.py:931 compares FP16 vs FP64 SDPA
math-backend outputs and expects them NOT to be close (assertRaisesRegex 'Tensor-likes are not
close'). On XPU, the oneDNN-based math backend keeps higher intermediate precision than CUDA's math
backend, so FP16 results are close enough to FP64 and no AssertionError is raised. This is a
CUDA-only accuracy assumption; XPU's higher precision is a feature, not a bug.

- No runtime change needed.
