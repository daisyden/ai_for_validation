## Root Cause

IGC reports SIMD32 register spills (up to 256 regs allocated, ~140+ spills) for many instantiations
of at::native::xpu::ReduceKernel<vt, ReduceOp<...>> defined in
third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/Reduce.h (used by ArgMin/ArgMax/Min/Max/Norm).
The vec_size=4 specializations for Half/BFloat16/float/long/int with ArgMinOps/ArgMaxOps cause heavy
register pressure due to large per-thread state (index+value pairs vectorized) and the unrolled
reduce loops in Reduce.h. NormOps SIMD32 also spills ~12 regs. No correctness issue, but spills hurt
perf.

## Fix Approach

- Tune Reduce.h: lower vt (vec_size) for argmin/argmax instantiations, force SIMD16 (sub_group_size 16) for high-register-pressure variants, split ReduceOp state into smaller fragments, or annotate kernels with [[intel::reqd_sub_group_size(16)]].
- Validate with IGC IGC_ShaderDumpEnable / register usage report.
- Coordinate with the kernels in ReduceMaxValuesKernels.cpp / ReduceMinValuesKernels.cpp / ReduceNormKernel.cpp.
