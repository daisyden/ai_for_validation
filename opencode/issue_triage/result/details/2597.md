## Root Cause

On BMG, INT4 GPTQ next/first-token latency is significantly worse than RTN/AWQ. LifengWang's oneDNN
trace shows GPTQ produces extra/different matmul shapes (e.g., 24x1x128:24x128x2056 and
24x1x2056:24x128 batched matmuls plus a 1x3072:3072x128256 LM-head shape) that RTN does not emit,
indicating GPTQ's per-channel/g-idx reordering is breaking weight-packing fusion and falling onto
unoptimized batched-matmul paths in oneDNN.

## Fix Approach

- Investigate torchao GPTQ XPU lowering: ensure the int4 weights + g_idx are packed into the same _weight_int4pack_mm_xpu layout as RTN (so oneDNN hits the optimized woq_matmul kernel), and eliminate the extra batched 24x1xK matmuls.
- Add the missing shapes to the oneDNN matmul primitive cache and benchmark on BMG.
