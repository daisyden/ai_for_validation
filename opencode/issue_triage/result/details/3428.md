## Root Cause

torch/_inductor/output_code.py hardcodes 'cuda' in compiled_graph.device_types / self.device_types
at three sites (lines 277, 594, 783) when deciding whether to log cudagraph skip reasons, so XPU
graphs never trigger the logging path. Upstream PR pytorch/pytorch#180971 (commit 5c63727703)
generalizes these to is_gpu()-style checks. The bug lives entirely in pytorch/pytorch, not in
torch-xpu-ops.

## Fix Approach

- Cherry-pick upstream commit 5c63727703819f36e077fc5392c3302ba377bcd8 (PR #180971) which replaces the three '"cuda" in ...device_types' checks in torch/_inductor/output_code.py with is_gpu()/non-CUDA-aware predicates.
- After cherry-pick, re-run the reproducer in the issue (cuda_check_count==0, is_gpu_count>0) to confirm.
- No torch-xpu-ops change required.
