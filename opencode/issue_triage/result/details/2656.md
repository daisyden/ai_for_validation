## Root Cause

Four timm models fail accuracy on BMG (WSL2 and native Linux) under inductor training:
sebotnet33ts_256 (amp_bf16), xcit_large_24_p8_224 (amp_fp16/fp16), mixer_b16_224 (fp16), fbnetv3_b
(bf16). Failures are in BatchNorm running_var / conv weight grads with RMSE ~3-4x reference;
reproduces in 2.10 and persists into 2.11. Disabling TORCHINDUCTOR_ONLINE_SOFTMAX does not help,
indicating the regression is in inductor codegen / Triton-XPU low-precision reduction accumulation
rather than online softmax.

## Fix Approach

- Bisect inductor/Triton-XPU for the BN reduction codegen path.
- ensure fp32 accumulator for BN running stats and conv-grad reductions under amp/bf16/fp16.
- Compare emitted Triton kernels for BN backward between CUDA and XPU.
- File upstream Triton-XPU issue if accumulator dtype is downcast.
