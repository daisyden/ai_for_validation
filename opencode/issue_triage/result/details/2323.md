## Root Cause

Feature request (PT2.15) for MOE training in TorchAO on XPU. The core primitive
(`_scaled_grouped_mm`) has no XPU dispatch — see
aten/src/ATen/native/native_functions.yaml:7402-7411 where only CUDA/CompositeExplicitAutograd
kernels are registered; the oneDNN team has a pending JIRA to add scaled grouped GEMM, which blocks
enabling the TorchAO MOE training recipes and UTs on XPU.

## Fix Approach

- Drive the oneDNN scaled_group_gemm primitive to completion, then register `_scaled_grouped_mm` / `_scaled_grouped_mm_v2` XPU dispatches in native_functions.yaml and implement them in torch-xpu-ops (e.g. `src/ATen/native/xpu/ScaledGroupedMM.cpp`) wrapping the oneDNN calls.
- Enable MOE training UTs from torchao and wire them into Inductor-XPU-UT-Data CI.
