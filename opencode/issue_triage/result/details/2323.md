## Root Cause

Feature request to enable MoE training on XPU via torchao. Requires scaled_group_gemm primitive
which is not yet supported in oneDNN — Intel JIRA MFDNN-14703 filed against oneDNN team. Without
scaled_group_gemm, MoE training cannot dispatch to XPU.

## Fix Approach

- Feature gap.
- Wait for oneDNN MFDNN-14703 (scaled_group_gemm).
- then implement XPU _scaled_grouped_mm dispatch and enable torchao MoE training UTs.
- Coordinate with Poland team for torchao integration.
