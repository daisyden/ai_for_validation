## Root Cause

Same TP transformer test path: missing DTensor sharding strategy for the XPU-dispatched fused
attention op causes propagation to fall back/abort, leaving an attention output as a plain
torch.Tensor while other activations are DTensors; the next aten.add.Tensor then mixes Tensor and
DTensor and DTensor's dispatch raises 'got mixed torch.Tensor and DTensor' from
torch/distributed/tensor/_dispatch.py. The underlying gap is the same as #1556 —
_scaled_dot_product_fused_attention_overrideable lacks a sharding rule (see
torch/distributed/tensor/_ops/_matrix_ops.py).

## Fix Approach

- Resolve by registering the sharding strategy for _scaled_dot_product_fused_attention_overrideable(_backward) upstream (duplicate/blocked by #1556).
- After the strategy is registered, DTensor will produce DTensor outputs from attention and the subsequent residual-add will no longer mix types.
- Track as duplicate of #1556.
