## Root Cause

test_aot_compile.TestAOTCompile.test_aot_compile_with_aoti runs CompiledAOTI.__post_init__ in
torch/_inductor/output_code.py. Historically only 'cuda' and 'cpu' branches existed, so
device_type='xpu' fell into `raise RuntimeError(f"unsupported device type {self.device_type}")` (now
at line 985). Although an xpu branch has since been added (line 969 dispatches to
torch._C._aoti.AOTIModelContainerRunnerXpu), the referenced PR 170056 is the in-flight XPU
enablement that has not yet landed on the tested branch.

## Fix Approach

- Ensure torch._inductor.output_code.CompiledAOTI.__post_init__ handles `device_type.startswith('xpu')` by instantiating AOTIModelContainerRunnerXpu (present upstream at torch/csrc/inductor/aoti_runner/*).
- Verify the binding is compiled into libtorch_xpu (check torch/csrc/inductor/aoti_runner/model_container_runner_xpu.cpp is built).
- Enable/unskip test_aot_compile_with_aoti on XPU once pytorch/pytorch#170056 lands.
