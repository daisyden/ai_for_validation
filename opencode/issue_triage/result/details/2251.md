## Root Cause

test_fake_autocast_* compares FakeTensor autocast output dtype against a real XPU run. For
linalg.pinv / pinverse the XPU autocast policy is wrong: these ops are on CUDA's AutocastCUDA fp32
cast list but XPU's AutocastXPU registration (third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/autocast_mode.cpp /
at::autocast::kernel registrations) does not include linalg_pinv and pinverse in the fp32-promote
set, so under torch.autocast(device_type='xpu', dtype=float16) the real op runs in fp16 while the
FakeTensor meta path (copied from CUDA policy) keeps it fp32 — producing 'Dtypes torch.float32 and
torch.float16 are not equal!'. Duplicate of #1963.

## Fix Approach

- Add linalg_pinv and pinverse (and their _out/_ex variants) to the XPU fp32 autocast promote list in third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/autocast_mode.cpp using KERNEL_XPU(..., fp32) to mirror AutocastCUDA in aten/src/ATen/autocast_mode.cpp.
- Re-run test_fake_autocast_linalg_pinv_xpu_float32 / test_fake_autocast_pinverse_xpu_float32 to confirm.
