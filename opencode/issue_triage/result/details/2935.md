## Root Cause

XLNetLMHeadModel huggingface training regresses 11% (float16) / 27% (amp_fp16) inductor-only on PVC
Max 1550 between release/2.10 and 2.11. XLNet exercises einsum/bmm/softmax fused triton kernels in
the attention block; the magnitude (>20%) on fp16 training points to a triton-xpu codegen or
inductor fusion/recomputation regression in the backward pass. No torch-xpu-ops aten kernel cited.

## Fix Approach

- Capture inductor output_code.py for fwd+bwd on both versions.
- diff fused attention/softmax triton kernels and num_warps/BLOCK sizes.
- Bisect both pytorch (release/2.11 vs 2.10) and intel-xpu-backend-for-triton between the two pinned commits.
- If isolated to triton, file with intel/intel-xpu-backend-for-triton.
- otherwise revert/tune the inductor pass. >5% perf drop on training → P0.
