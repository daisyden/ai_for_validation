## Root Cause

BF16/FP16 accuracy gap in test_block_addmm for the BSR addmm path. The XPU block-sparse addmm kernel
is implemented in
third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/sparse/xpu/sycl/SparseCsrTensorMathKernels.cpp, which
performs reduced-precision accumulation for bf16/fp16 dense fragments leading to tolerances above
the upstream test thresholds for block_size 2/3 noncontiguous variants.

## Fix Approach

- Promote the inner-product accumulator to float32 in the BSR addmm SYCL kernel (mirror CUDA's accumulator dtype), or relax tolerances via xpu-specific dtype-tolerance entries.
- rerun test_block_addmm with bf16/fp16 across block_size 2/3 contiguous and noncontiguous to confirm and unskip in test_sparse_csr_xpu skip list.
