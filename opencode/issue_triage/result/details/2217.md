## Root Cause

Meta-tracking issue for AO performance regressions and gaps on XPU. Two concrete workstreams: (1)
GEMM-related performance regression tracked in #2202 (MFDNN-14295); (2) BF16 slower than FP16 during
model execution on BMG due to suboptimal oneDNN BF16 GEMM primitives (MFDNN-14324/-13596; fix
present in PTL but missing on BMG). Root cause resides in oneDNN BF16 matmul kernel selection/tuning
for BMG rather than any specific torch-xpu-ops file.

## Fix Approach

- Drive the listed oneDNN tickets to closure: request oneDNN team to improve BF16 matmul perf on BMG to match FP16/PTL, and merge the GEMM regression fix from #2202.
- In torch-xpu-ops, re-benchmark matmul paths in src/ATen/native/xpu/mkldnn/Matmul.cpp after new oneDNN drops and remove any BF16-specific workarounds once upstream is fixed.
