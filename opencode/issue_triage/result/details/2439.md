## Root Cause

TestDecompXPU.test_quick_addmv_xpu_float64 fails with greatest abs diff 9e-7 (tol 1e-7) on 1 of 5
elements after upgrading oneDNN 3.9 -> 3.10. addmv on XPU is dispatched through oneDNN matmul
(third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/Blas.cpp); oneDNN 3.10 changed FP64 matmul
accumulation/order producing slightly different rounding vs decomposition reference. Same case is
tracked in #2269 with upstream fix pytorch/pytorch#174590 raising tolerance for addmv decomp.
