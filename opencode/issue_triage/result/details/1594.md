## Root Cause

Tracking issue for SYCL compile-time warnings in torch-xpu-ops: deprecated at::xpu::HostAlloc /
CachingHostAllocator_recordEvent usage in src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/MultiTensorApply.h:170,191;
unused variables in Dequant_int4.cpp:33 and SparseSoftmaxKernels.cpp:383,395,399; -Wtype-limits in
PSTLFunctions.h:360; uninitialized out_cl_h_stride in DilatedMaxPool3d.cpp:259; plus upstream
warnings from sycl/detail/builtins/builtins.hpp and sycl/accessor.hpp. Most items already checked
off.

## Fix Approach

- Continue to close remaining unchecked warnings as they appear in the nightly build log.
- migrate deprecated host-allocator APIs to at::getHostAllocator(at::kXPU).
- remove unused variables and fix uninitialized writes.
- Upstream SYCL header warnings (intel/llvm#15063, accessor.hpp throw/terminate) are filed against the compiler and can only be suppressed locally.
