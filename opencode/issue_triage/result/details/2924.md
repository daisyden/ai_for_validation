## Root Cause

Training accuracy failure on xcit_large_24_p8_224 amp_bf16 (grad RMSE for
patch_embed.proj.0.0.weight 0.006 vs ref 0.0007) that reproduces on Max 1100 but not Max 1550. Same
underlying cause as #2928: pytorch/pytorch PR #171773 switched Inductor to tl.sqrt_rn, which IGC
mis-optimizes. Additionally jianyizh found that sycl::rsqrt in
third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/comm/XPUMathCompat.h:36,39 contributes; replacing it with 1.f/::sqrt
plus -cl-opt-disable makes the test pass. So two math paths disagree between eager (rsqrt) and
inductor/triton (sqrt_rn) under IGC.

## Fix Approach

- Align the eager and Inductor math paths: (1) in third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/comm/XPUMathCompat.h replace sycl::rsqrt with 1.f/sycl::sqrt (or an equivalent precise variant) so eager matches CUDA/Triton semantics.
- (2) in Inductor XPU codegen, fall back from tl.sqrt_rn to libdevice.sqrt until the IGC fix (intel-xpu-backend-for-triton#6261) lands.
- (3) audit the other math intrinsics in XPUMathCompat.h for eager-vs-Triton consistency as jianyizh noted.
- Re-enable after driver fix.
