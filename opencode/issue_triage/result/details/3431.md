## Root Cause

`torch.fft.rfft` on an XPU bfloat16 tensor raises `Unsupported dtype BFloat16`. The dtype check
lives in upstream `aten/src/ATen/native/SpectralOps.cpp:82-91` (`promote_type_fft`): when device is
CUDA/XPU/meta, only kHalf/kFloat/kDouble are accepted, and bfloat16 is neither promoted nor
whitelisted. Upstream PR pytorch/pytorch#180766 is the proposed fix that promotes bf16 to float32
for FFT.

## Fix Approach

- Land/cherry-pick upstream pytorch/pytorch#180766 to extend `promote_type_fft` in SpectralOps.cpp to promote BFloat16 → Float for CUDA/XPU.
- Add a torch-xpu-ops UT in test/xpu covering rfft/fft/irfft with bfloat16 input.
