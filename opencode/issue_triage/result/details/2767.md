AssociativeScanTests) under third_party/torch-xpu-ops/test/xpu/functorch/test_control_flow_xpu.py

upstream pytorch (torch/_higher_order_ops/, torch/_dynamo/variables/higher_order_ops.py), not in any
XPU SYCL kernel. Tests are currently skipped to keep CI green.
