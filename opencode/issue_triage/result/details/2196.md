## Root Cause

DistributionElementwiseKernelFunctor
(third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/DistributionTemplates.h:64) when instantiated
with Normal2DistributionFunctor + NormalTransformFunctor (unroll factor 2) for Half/BFloat16/float
produces SIMD32 kernels allocating 128 regs with ~4 regs spilled. The spill originates from carrying
the full PhiloxState plus two accumulated scalar+acc_type pairs across the unrolled iterations in
the operator() (unroll=2), slightly exceeding the large-GRF threshold.

## Fix Approach

- Minor tuning only: either (a) drop the unroll factor from 2 to 1 for Normal (and other 2-output distributions) to halve per-thread state, or (b) hoist the philox state update out of the unrolled loop and reuse a single per-item register, or (c) add intel::reqd_sub_group_size(32) and large_grf_kernel attribute.
- Low priority since only ~4 regs spill.
- impact on throughput is marginal but trivial to eliminate.
