## Root Cause

IGC reports register spill (SIMD32 allocated 128 regs and spilled around 4) when compiling
DistributionElementwiseKernelFunctor for Half/BFloat16/float Normal distribution paths. The functor
at third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/DistributionTemplates.h:64 (used at lines
163,175) carries large state (philox engine state, transform/distribution functors,
OffsetCalculator) plus accumulator-typed math, exceeding the 128-reg budget per SIMD32 work item.
This is a code-generation/perf cleanliness issue, not a correctness bug.

## Fix Approach

- Refactor DistributionElementwiseKernelFunctor in DistributionTemplates.h to reduce live state: store philox seed/offset by value (regenerate state per item), inline/strip the OffsetCalculator for contiguous fast-path, or split the templated body so per-item temporaries don't survive across the unrolled VEC loop.
- Re-evaluate spill warning under SIMD16/SIMD32 after refactor.
