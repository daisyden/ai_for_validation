## Root Cause

Random/flaky failure in functorch test_vjpvjp_index_reduce_prod_xpu_float32
(test/xpu/functorch/test_ops_xpu.py) exercising the double-backward of index_reduce with
reduce='prod'. The XPU implementation lives in
third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/Indexing.cpp (index_reduce kernels);
non-determinism comes from atomic prod accumulation order producing slight numerical noise that
occasionally exceeds vjpvjp tolerance. wpietka has a fix in progress.

## Fix Approach

- Land wpietka's fix (likely deterministic accumulation or bumped tolerance / torch.testing tol entry for index_reduce prod).
- Until merged, keep the case in the skip list.
- Verify via repeated runs (>=20) that flakiness is gone.
