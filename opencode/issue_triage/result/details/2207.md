## Root Cause

Feature-tracking issue for FP8 (e4m3fn/e5m2/e8m0fnu/fnuz variants) and MXFP8/MXFP4/NVFP4 op
enablement on XPU to reach CUDA parity. Many memory ops (fill, flip, index, gather, cat, eq/ne,
where, empty/zeros/ones, to, copy, clone) and GEMM ops (_scaled_mm tensorwise/channelwise/blockwise,
_scaled_grouped_mm) are missing or partial. The gating items now are copy support for Float8_e8m0fnu
and Float4_e2m1fn_x2, and block-scaled _scaled_mm (targeting 2.12+).

## Fix Approach

- Continue landing the referenced PRs (#2145 arithmetic, #2152 compare/cat/where, #2154 compare, #2190 flip/index, #2258 copy) and add AT_DISPATCH macros for Float8_e8m0fnu / Float4_e2m1fn_x2 in src/ATen/native/xpu/Copy.cpp and related memory-op kernels.
- For _scaled_mm block-scaled variants, extend src/ATen/native/xpu/Blas.cpp to call the oneDNN matmul primitive with scale attributes once oneDNN/XeTLA exposes them.
- open separate sub-issues for NVFP4 (E5M3 scale) and _scaled_grouped_mm targeting PT 2.16.
