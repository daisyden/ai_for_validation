## Root Cause

Tracking issue to enable FP8/MXFP8 dtype coverage (Float8_e4m3fn, e5m2, e8m0fnu, Float4_e2m1fn_x2)
across pointwise/index/copy/compare/_scaled_mm ops in torch-xpu-ops, aligning with CUDA. Many
sub-ops (fill, flip, index*, gather, cat, eq, where, copy) require dtype dispatch macros in
src/ATen/native/xpu/*.cpp and corresponding SYCL kernels under src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/ to be
extended (e.g., Copy.cpp, IndexingKernels.cpp). _scaled_mm path lives in oneDNN-backed BlasImpl.
Most 2.10 scope already landed via #2152/#2190/#2258; remaining items (block scaling,
_scaled_grouped_mm, MXFP4) are deferred.

## Fix Approach

- Extend AT_DISPATCH macros in the relevant XPU op .cpp files to include Float8_e8m0fnu and Float4_e2m1fn_x2.
- add dtype to SYCL kernels (Copy.cpp first per @carsonwang request).
- For _scaled_mm block/MX scaling and _scaled_grouped_mm, coordinate with oneDNN team.
- Continue tracking sub-PRs and align with CUDA op-list in pytorch/pytorch/aten/src/ATen/native/cuda/Blas.cpp.
