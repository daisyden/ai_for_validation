fail with 'Tensor-likes are not equal Mismatched 3/6 (50%)' on argmax tie-breaking — the compiled

tracked as driver-side bug GSD-11415 (issue labelled 'dependency component: driver'). The test
expects deterministic first-index-of-max behavior that the current driver/Triton lowering does not
honor for the duplicate/NaN cases. Reproducer pinned to pytorch/pytorch#170056.
