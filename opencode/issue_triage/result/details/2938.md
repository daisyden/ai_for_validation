## Root Cause

basic_gnn_gin / basic_gnn_sage fp32 inference regress ~25% in inductor only (eager ratio 1.00,
inductor 0.75) on torchbench between 2.10 and 2.11 (PVC Max 1100, triton-xpu 3.7.0). The
eager-vs-inductor split isolates the regression to torch.compile codegen — likely
scatter/gather/index_add/SpMM-like triton kernels emitted for GNN message-passing whose
tile/num_warps heuristic regressed, or an inductor pre-grad/decomp change. Not a torch-xpu-ops aten
kernel issue.

## Fix Approach

- Reproduce with TORCH_LOGS=+inductor,output_code on 2.10 and 2.11.
- diff generated triton kernels for the scatter/gather ops dominating GNN.
- Bisect intel-xpu-backend-for-triton and pytorch inductor commits between branches. >5% perf drop → P0.
- coordinate with triton-xpu team to restore heuristic or pin a known-good autotune config.
