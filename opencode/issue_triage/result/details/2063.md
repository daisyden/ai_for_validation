## Root Cause

Internal comments/identifiers in torch-xpu-ops still use vendor-specific or legacy terminology such
as 'Tile' and 'SIMD' (e.g. src/comm/DeviceProperties.h:109 'syclMaxWorkItemsPerTile', widespread
'SIMD' references in kernels) that are not part of the SYCL 2020 language model and confuse new
contributors. This is a pure code-hygiene request by EikanWang with no labels other than
'enhancement' and no associated test failure.

## Fix Approach

- Enumerate terms (Tile, SIMD, possibly EU, subslice) and mechanically rename to SYCL-conformant equivalents (work-group / sub-group / compute-unit) in both comments and symbol names, updating call sites.
- Keep public/ABI-visible names stable or add deprecation aliases where needed.
- Land as a series of small refactor PRs.
- Defer until higher-priority UT work settles.
