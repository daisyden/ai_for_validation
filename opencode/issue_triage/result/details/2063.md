identifiers, comments, and kernel names — concepts that are either implementation details we don't
intend to expose, or are not part of the SYCL language. This is a code-hygiene/rename task with no
functional impact.

## Fix Approach

- Audit src/ for occurrences of 'Tile' and 'SIMD' (and any other non-SYCL terms surfaced later).
- rename to SYCL-canonical terms (e.g., sub_group, work_group, vec width) in identifiers and comments.
