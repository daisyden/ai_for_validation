## Root Cause

test_autocast_flash_attention at test/dynamo/test_activation_checkpointing.py:1810 calls
`torch.ops.aten._scaled_dot_product_efficient_attention.default` under
`torch.autocast(device_type='xpu')` plus reentrant checkpointing, then compares eager vs
`aot_eager_decomp_partition` outputs at fp32 default tolerances (atol=1e-5, rtol=1.3e-6). The XPU
implementation in third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/transformers/Attention.cpp:341
(`_scaled_dot_product_efficient_attention_xpu`) dispatches to the SYCL kernel in
src/ATen/native/transformers/sycl/AttentionKernels.cpp; under autocast it runs in bf16/fp16, so a
0.0011 absolute / 0.285 relative drift on 94% of the 4096 elements is within accumulator precision
but blows fp32 tolerance. Upstream tracking: pytorch/pytorch#169241.
