## Root Cause

Enhancement/tracking issue requesting evaluation of register spill warnings emitted by the SYCL
compiler for many vectorized elementwise and distribution SYCL kernels under
third_party/torch-xpu-ops/src/ATen/native/xpu/sycl/ (e.g., VectorizedElementwiseKernel,
DistributionElementwiseKernelFunctor in BesselY0Kernel.cpp, DistributionKernels.cpp). The compiler
logs show warnings like 'compiled SIMD32 allocated 128 regs and spilled around N', indicating
sub-optimal register usage that may impact occupancy/perf but does not cause functional failures.
Maintainer (fengyuan14) marked it as low-priority, last-mile peak-performance tuning.
